  • May 1996

    To implement the Sex Discrimination Ordinance (SDO) and the Disability Discrimination Ordinance (DDO) – Hong Kong’s first two anti-discrimination ordinances – the Equal Opportunities Commission (EOC) was established in May 1996, with Prof Fanny CHEUNG Mui-ching as the Commission’s first Chairperson.
    The employment-related provisions of the SDO and DDO later came into effect on 20 September 1996, while the non-employment-related provisions became effective on 20 December 1996.
